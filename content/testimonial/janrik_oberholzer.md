I've had the pleasure of working alongside Maxwell Jensen at Noroff Education,
and I can say without hesitation that he's one of the most dedicated and
impactful educators I’ve had the privilege to work with.

Maxwell originally joined Noroff as a student in the Network and System
Administration program, where he stood out immediately for his technical skill
and drive. It wasn’t long before we welcomed him to the teaching team and
that’s when his real impact began.

Maxwell took the lead in overhauling our Linux course from the ground up. He
rewrote the curriculum, re-recorded lectures, and completely restructured the
course to make it clearer, more relevant, and more engaging. Thanks to his
work, Linux has become a student favourite regularly praised for its clarity
and depth.

What sets Maxwell apart is his ability to take complex ideas and turn them into
lessons that students genuinely enjoy. His work ethic, passion for teaching,
and technical expertise make him an incredible asset to any team.

While I'm sad to see him move on, I know he'll thrive wherever he goes. Any
organization would be lucky to have him.
